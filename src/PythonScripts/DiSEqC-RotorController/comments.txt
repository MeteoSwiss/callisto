anypos.py allows to point at any sidereal position given by right-ascension (ra) and declination(dec)
For proper alignment the referecne values 180 for azimuth and 45 for elevation must be edited, ideally
after alignment of the telescope on the Sun

goto.py allows to position the telescpe on terrestrial coordinates.
For proper alignment the referecne values 180 for azimuth and 45 for elevation must be edited, ideally
after alignment of the telescope on the Sun
